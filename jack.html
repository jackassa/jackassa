Common Agriculture Terms

Agroecology

The study of ecological processes applied to agricultural production systems. Agroecology aims to create sustainable and environmentally friendly farming practices.

Crop Rotation

The practice of growing different types of crops in the same area across a sequence of seasons. It helps in improving soil health and reducing pests and diseases.

Irrigation

The artificial application of water to soil to assist in the growing of crops. It is crucial in regions where rainfall is insufficient to meet the needs of the crops.

Organic Farming

A farming system that uses environmentally friendly methods, avoiding synthetic chemicals, and focusing on maintaining ecological balance and soil fertility.

Photosynthesis

The process by which green plants use sunlight to synthesize foods from carbon dioxide and water. Photosynthesis is essential for the growth of plants.
